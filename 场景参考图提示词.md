# SELF DISTILL — 场景参考图提示词

> **用途**：在开始视频片段生成之前，先用以下提示词生成各场景的环境参考图，锁定空间设计、道具位置、光线方向与色调风格。参考图生成后作为 Image Reference 输入后续视频生成（Runway Gen-4 reference image / Kling style reference / ComfyUI img2img）。

---

## 场景总览

| 编号 | 场景名称 | 对应 Shot | 状态说明 |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| SCENE 1 | 客服接待台 | 02 / 03 / 07 / 10 | Maya 的工作环境，贯穿 Act 1–3 |
| SCENE 2A | 白领格子间（混乱版） | 04 / 08 | 文件堆满桌，Act 1–2 |
| SCENE 2B | 白领格子间（空置版） | 11 | 文件消失，椅子空转，Act 3 |
| SCENE 3A | 教室（学生分心版） | 05 / 09 | 学生各行其是，Act 1–2 |
| SCENE 3B | 教室（学生专注版） | 12 | 白板自动授课，学生专注，Act 3 |
| SCENE 4 | 海滩 | 13 / 14 / 15 / 16 / 17 | 金色午后，三把沙滩椅，Act 4–5 |

---

## 生成建议流程

1. 每个场景用以下提示词生成 **4–6 张候选图**
2. 从候选图中筛选最符合设计意图的 **1 张**作为场景基准图
3. 记录该图的 Seed（种子值），后续同场景所有镜头使用**相同 Seed**
4. 在 Runway / Kling 中将基准图设为 **First Frame Reference** 或 **Scene Reference**
5. 涉及同一场景的不同状态（如 2A↔2B、3A↔3B），在基准图基础上用 **img2img / inpainting** 修改状态

### 提示词 — 空场景版（无人物）

```
Photorealistic empty interior, corporate customer service reception desk, no people present.

ENVIRONMENT: Modern open-plan office reception area. Light gray fabric cubicle divider panels (120cm tall, slightly worn). Desk surface: dark gray laminate, completely covered with stacked manila folders in chaotic piles (15-20cm height, edges misaligned, some folders slightly open). Two black corded landline telephones — one on the left side of the desk, one on the right — phone cords trailing loosely across the desk surface. A standard black-framed 24-inch computer monitor displaying 6-8 overlapping green notification popup windows (each reads "New Message", urgent, chaotic layering). A black over-ear customer service headset resting on the desk near the right phone.

BACKGROUND: Shallow depth of field, blurred open-plan office behind the reception desk — soft ghost shapes of other workstations, filing cabinets, a water cooler. No identifiable faces or text. Background is 60% blurred.

WINDOW LIGHT: Cold white daylight entering from the LEFT side of frame (window itself not visible, only its directional light). Light falls at approximately 45° downward from the upper left, casting soft directional shadows to the right across the desk.

LIGHTING:
- Key: cool white 5600K, upper-left 45°, simulates overcast window daylight
- Fill: gentle diffused white light from the right at 1/3 key intensity, prevents hard shadows
- Overhead: flat white fluorescent ceiling panels providing low-level ambient base
- Practical: computer monitor screen casts a subtle green glow across the desk surface

COLOR GRADE: Cold and corporate. 4800K effective color temperature. Elevated contrast. Subtle cyan-green tint in shadow areas. Light 8mm film grain texture overlaid. Slight vignette darkening at frame corners.

CAMERA: Eye-level, approximately 150cm high. Slight 15° angle — positioned from the customer-facing side, looking across the reception desk toward the monitor and cubicle panels. Desk occupies the lower half of frame. The monitor and upper cubicle panels occupy the upper half.

LENS FEEL: Leica Summilux-C 25mm prime equivalent — slightly wide, natural perspective, no distortion, shallow focus at background.

MOOD: Corporate, slightly oppressive, controlled chaos. Like a still photograph from a 1960s public service announcement about workplace stress, shot with modern cinematic equipment.

STYLE: Photorealistic. No illustration, no stylization, no cartoon. Film grain yes. HDR no.

ASPECT RATIO: 16:9
```

OVERHEAD LIGHTING: White fluorescent tube panels in a dropped ceiling grid, providing flat, slightly harsh overhead illumination. The lighting has a faint industrial quality.

- Primary: flat fluorescent overhead, 5500K, slightly harsh and directionless

LENS FEEL: Leica Summilux-C 50mm prime equivalent — natural, slightly compressed perspective. Background cubicles softly out of focus.

MOOD: Suffocating, absurdly cluttered. The desk should look genuinely overwhelmed — the kind of mess that happens when a person has been drowning in paperwork for months and stopped trying.

STYLE: Photorealistic cinematic. Film grain. No stylization.

Photorealistic empty interior, open-plan office cubicle, desk completely clear, no people.

MOOD: Comic and slightly eerie. The absence of the person should feel like relief,
not tragedy — but an uncanny relief. The desk is suspiciously, impossibly clean.

STYLE: Photorealistic. Film grain. No stylization.

STYLE: Photorealistic cinematic. 1960s PSA sensibility with modern production.
